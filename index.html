About Us

Netwire Limited is a Ghanaian owned private company limited by shares and duly incorporated under the laws of the Republic of Ghana. The company was founded by a serial entrepreneur and a technology professional who has extensive knowledge of the financial sector backed by a strong Advisory Board and a management team of experienced financial services, operations and IT professionals.

Mission

To put a simple, fast, easy to use, network agnostic and secure payment application in the hands of consumers wherever they do commerce.

Vision

By leveraging innovation and ingenuity to become the most reliable digital financial services provider and e-commerce enabler in Ghana and its neighboring West African countries.

Values

To create a thriving culture and environment of diligence, commitment, integrity and respect to provide innovative products that bring a positive impact on our society.

Services

Netwire Limited is a Turnkey solutions provider and a specialist software vendor mainly focused on promoting financial inclusion and commerce. Backed by some of the world’s leading players in Financial Inclusion players across Africa and Asia Pacific, our focus remains primarily in Digital Financial Services. With highly trained and knowledgeable resources, Netwire’s single-mindedness allows it to constantly innovate new ideas and services together with our clients to help create competitive and usable products.

EasyPay E-Wallet

Netwire EasyPay is a USSD and Mobile App based Digital Financial Service (DFS) which uses QR codes for transfer and payments. The EasyPay is a combination of USSD and mobile application-based wallet platform that can be used by feature/ÿam" phone and also by Smartphone of Android or iOS operating system.

Omni-Channel Payments

Netwire OmniPay a one-stop (omnichannel) solution for merchants payment acquiring and multiple free software solutions through the cloud to enable them to quickly serve their business automation needs. OmniPay goes beyond simply online or in-person payments and also includes invoicing, recurring billing and mobile payments.

Micro-Lending

This product is designed to meet the needs and aspiration of the unbanked. The target individuals are artisans and retailers who earns income daily, weekly or periodically. It is evident that commercial banks have not given enough attention to personal and household sector as well as the informal sector when it comes to loan portfolio management.

Turnkey Solutions

Customer focus means giving customers exactly what they need, both products and services. To achieve this, we always listen to our customers to understand their requirements and develop appropriate “tailormade” solutions. This involves also providing expert advice in all areas of the projects to ensure that our customers receive no less than what they need, and don't end up paying for more.
